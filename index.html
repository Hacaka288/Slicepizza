🍕 How to Play

1

One player cuts the pizza by dragging across it. You have 15 seconds to make as many cuts as you want. Each cut creates a new slice.

2

The other player selects first. they can pick any slice they want.

3

Players alternate picking slices until the pizza is gone. Click the percentage tags below the pizza. You can pick a tag that correlates with the colored slice you want.

4

The goal of the game is simple. The player with the most pizza % wins!
